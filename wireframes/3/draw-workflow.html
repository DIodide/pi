Why This Fits PI

Natural language beats filters

Personal constraints

Housing is full of tradeoffs students describe conversationally: "near my friends but quiet" or "single unless bathroom is terrible."

Deadline awareness

Listserv and Forum notices can feed PI reminders about housing sessions, form deadlines, and draw windows.

High-trust writes

PI should never submit housing choices silently. The UI makes confirmation and audit history first-class.
